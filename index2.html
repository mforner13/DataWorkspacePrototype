Data at DIT

The DIT Data Workspace shows the different datasets that the department creates, uses and publishes.

Data Hub data

Data Hub is DIT's central system for managing, tracking and building relationships with UK and international businesses.

HMRC Data

Statistics that record the movement - for trade purposes - of goods between the UK and both EU and non-EU countries.

One List

The One List is an internal management tool that supports the account management of over 1700 key companies.

ONS Data

The Office for National Statistics is the UK's recognised national statistical institute and is its largest producer of official statistics.

Open datasets

Datasets published by a range of national and international organisations that are free for use, re-use and redistribution.

Open datasets (DIT)

Data published by the Department for International Trade for public use.

Reference Datasets

Reference data are data that define the set of permissible values to be used by other data fields
