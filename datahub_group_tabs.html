Data Hub data

Data Hub is DIT's central system for managing, tracking and building relationships with UK and international businesses. It allows DIT staff and partners to record information about the companies and contacts they meet, the interactions and services provided to them, as well as tracking investment projects and Overseas Market Introduction Service (OMIS) orders.


Contents

  • Raw datasets

Raw datasets

These are raw datasets for use in analytical tools.

Data Hub companies

Records of companies and other organisations that DIT has interacted with.

Data Hub contacts

Records of business contacts that DIT staff have interacted with.

Data Hub events

Records of DIT events in the UK and overseas.

Data Hub interactions & service deliveries

Records of interactions between DIT staff and company contacts.

Data Hub investment projects

Data about investment projects, either into or within the UK, that DIT has supported or is aware of.

Overseas Market Introduction Service (OMIS) Orders

Records of Overseas Market Introduction Service (OMIS) orders delivered by DIT for UK companies.

Data cuts

These are cuts of of raw datasets taken from various internal and external sources.

Investments landed 2018-2019

FDI and non-FDI projects from Data Hub which landed in the 2018-2019 financial year.

Completed OMIS orders

All Overseas Market Introduction Service (OMIS) orders completed in the past 7 years.

Data Hub companies in the North East

Companies listed on Data Hub which are based in the North East of England.
