Overseas Market Introduction Service (OMIS) orders

Records of Overseas Market Introduction Service (OMIS) orders delivered by DIT for UK companies.

OMIS orders are orders for bespoke support for which UK businesses pay DIT. Orders are delivered by staff in overseas posts. Services include bespoke market research reports, introductions to new contacts and support in organising events and missions.

The data includes details of current and historic orders, with details of customers, services provided and fees charged.
