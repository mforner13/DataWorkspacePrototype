Data Hub Investments

Records of investment projects, either into or within the UK, that DIT has supported or is aware of.

Most of this data is about foreign direct investment (FDI) projects that DIT has actively supported. However, it also contains data on non-FDI projects, and FDI projects that DIT has not had active involvement in.

Project records include data about the foreign investor company, details of the project and its value, and details of DIT support provided.
